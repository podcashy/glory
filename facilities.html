Facilities

Building a Better Learning Environment for All Learners

Overview

Glory Elite Education Centre currently operates with basic but functional learning facilities that support quality education for our learners. However, due to increasing enrollment and community demand, we are actively seeking support to expand and modernize our infrastructure.

Our goal is to build a fully equipped, modern learning environment that will serve both the school and the wider Kayole community.

Classrooms

We currently have functional classrooms that support daily learning activities. These classrooms are used to accommodate learners across different levels of primary education.

However, as the number of learners continues to grow, there is an urgent need to construct additional modern classrooms that are spacious, well-ventilated, and fully equipped with learning materials.

Support in this area will directly improve learning conditions and ensure that no child is left behind due to limited space.

Computer Lab

We have introduced basic computer learning to equip learners with essential digital skills required in today’s world.

With proper funding, we aim to fully establish a modern ICT lab with computers, internet access, and digital learning tools to enhance CBC digital literacy requirements.

Library (Planned Development)

We plan to establish a fully functional school library that will provide learners with access to books, research materials, and a quiet study space.

This facility will promote reading culture, improve literacy levels, and support academic excellence among learners.

Water System (Borehole Project)

One of our most important planned projects is the installation of a borehole water system. This will ensure reliable access to clean and safe water for the school.

Beyond serving the school, the borehole will also benefit the surrounding community by providing affordable clean water access.

In addition, the water system will generate income for the school through controlled water sales. This income will help sustain school operations and support vulnerable learners who cannot afford full fees.

Support Our Growth

All the facilities listed above represent our vision for the future. However, we require financial and material support to fully realize these projects.

We welcome partners, donors, well-wishers, and organizations who would like to contribute toward improving education and community development in Kayole.

Your support will directly impact the lives of children, families, and the entire community.
