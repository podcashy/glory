Facilities

Building a Better Learning Environment for All Learners

Overview

Glory Elite Education Centre currently operates with basic but functional learning facilities that support quality education. However, we are actively seeking support to expand and modernize our infrastructure to meet increasing enrollment.

Classrooms

We currently have functional classrooms for learners across all levels. As we grow, there is an urgent need to construct additional modern classrooms that are spacious, well-ventilated, and fully equipped.

Computer Lab

We have introduced basic computer learning. With proper funding, we aim to establish a modern ICT lab with computers and internet access to enhance CBC digital literacy.

Library (Planned Development)

We plan to establish a fully functional school library to provide research materials and a quiet study space, promoting a strong reading culture.

Water System (Borehole Project)

One of our most critical projects is the borehole water system. Beyond serving the school, it will provide clean water access to the surrounding community and help sustain school operations.

Support Our Growth

Realizing these visions requires your support. We welcome partners, donors, and well-wishers to contribute toward improving education and community development in Kayole.
