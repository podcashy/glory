Academics

CBC Curriculum & Holistic Learning Approach

Overview

Glory Elite Education Centre follows the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), which focuses on nurturing learners’ talents, skills, creativity, and practical understanding of real-life situations.

We believe education should go beyond textbooks. Our teaching approach ensures that learners develop academically, socially, emotionally, and physically.

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)

Under the CBC system, learners are actively involved in hands-on activities, group work, problem-solving tasks, and interactive learning sessions. This helps them build confidence, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

Learning Areas

We offer a wide range of learning areas including:

  • English Language
  • Kiswahili Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science & Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical & Health Education
  • Computer Studies

Extracurricular Activities

In line with CBC requirements, we strongly support activities that help learners discover their talents and build teamwork, leadership, and confidence.

  • Sports (Football, Athletics)
  • Music, Dance & Drama
  • Clubs & Societies
  • Environmental Activities
  • Talent Shows

Educational Trips

Our learners participate in supervised educational trips to museums, parks, and industries. These trips enhance understanding of classroom concepts through real-life exposure.

Our Alumni

We are proud of our former learners who have progressed to secondary schools and colleges. Their success is a reflection of our strong academic foundation and discipline.
