Overview

Glory Elite Education Centre is committed to transforming lives through education in Kayole. However, we face major infrastructure and resource challenges that limit our ability to fully serve our learners and the wider community.

We are currently seeking support from well-wishers, donors, organizations, and partners to help us complete key development projects that will directly improve education quality and community welfare.

Our Development Projects

1. Construction of Modern Classrooms

Our current classrooms are functional but limited in space and infrastructure. Due to increasing enrollment, there is urgent need for additional modern classrooms.

The new classrooms will be spacious, well-ventilated, and properly equipped to support CBC learning, ensuring that every child learns in a safe and comfortable environment.

2. Establishment of Computer Laboratory

Digital literacy is a key requirement under CBC. We aim to establish a fully equipped computer lab with modern computers and internet access.

This will empower learners with essential ICT skills, preparing them for secondary education, higher learning, and the modern job market.

3. School Library Development

We plan to develop a fully functional library to promote reading culture and academic excellence.

The library will provide learners with access to textbooks, storybooks, reference materials, and a quiet environment for study and research.

4. Borehole Water Project

Access to clean water is a major challenge in our community. The borehole project will provide safe and reliable water for the school.

Beyond the school, the borehole will serve the surrounding community, improving health and living conditions.

Additionally, water distribution will generate sustainable income for the school, helping support operational costs and assist vulnerable learners.
