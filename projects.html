Overview

Glory Elite Education Centre is committed to transforming lives through education in Kayole. However, we face infrastructure challenges that limit our ability to fully serve our community.

We are seeking support from well-wishers and partners to help us complete key development projects that will directly improve education quality.

Our Development Projects

1. Construction of Modern Classrooms

New classrooms will be spacious and well-ventilated to support CBC learning, ensuring every child learns in a safe and comfortable environment.

2. Establishment of Computer Laboratory

We aim to establish a fully equipped lab to empower learners with essential ICT skills, preparing them for the modern world.

3. School Library Development

The library will provide access to storybooks and reference materials, promoting a strong reading culture and academic excellence.

4. Borehole Water Project

This project will provide safe water for the school and the community, while also generating sustainable income to assist vulnerable learners.
